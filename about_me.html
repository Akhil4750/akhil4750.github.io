About Me

Welcome to my professional space! I'm Akhil Varma Tirumalaraju, a passionate and driven individual with a strong foundation in both Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science. My journey from the small town of Vizianagaram, Andhra Pradesh, to pursuing advanced studies and working on impactful projects across various domains, has shaped me into the versatile professional I am today.

My Journey

I began my academic journey in Vizianagaram, where I completed my schooling. My curiosity and drive led me to Aditya Engineering College (AEC) in Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh, where I pursued a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering. My dedication to academic excellence earned me a spot in the top ranks throughout my undergraduate studies, culminating in a distinction upon graduation.

During my time at AEC, I actively engaged in both professional and community service organizations. I was a member of the Student - Indian Society of Mechanical Engineering (Stud.ISME) and the Society of Automotive Engineering. One of the highlights of my undergraduate years was participating in a student Formula car competition, where I collaborated with a team of 16 to design, fabricate, and race our vehicle, securing second place. My role in the design and sourcing team was pivotal to our success.

I also served as a board member, Treasurer, and Advisor in the Aditya LEO Club, where I led various community service initiatives, including blood donation camps, fundraising events, and remodeling primary schools. These experiences not only honed my leadership and organizational skills but also instilled in me a deep sense of responsibility toward societal well-being.

Professional Experience

After graduation, I joined Wipro in Bangalore, where I worked on two major projects as detailed in my resume. Despite being new to the IT field, I quickly adapted to the demands of software development, mastering coding and various development tools. The experience at Wipro, guided by its core values, equipped me with essential professional skills and a strong understanding of corporate dynamics.

My time at Wipro fueled my desire to deepen my knowledge in Computer Science, leading me to pursue a Master's degree in the field. The challenges and opportunities I encountered during this phase of my life further solidified my confidence and passion for technology.

Life in the United States

Moving to the United States for my Master's at the University of Bridgeport was a transformative experience. The diverse and dynamic environment here pushed me to explore new avenues and discover my full potential. One of the most rewarding experiences was emceeing the International Festival, which had an audience of over 350 students, and leading orientations for international students every semester. My involvement in these activities earned me the Graduate Assistantship in the Center for Student Involvement.

My commitment to thinking outside the box and taking on additional responsibilities led to being named Graduate Assistant of the Year. Furthermore, my significant impact on the university community was recognized with the Shining Knights Spirit Award at the Student Leadership Awards.

Transferable Qualities

Throughout my journey, several key qualities have consistently driven my success:

  • Adaptability: Transitioning from Mechanical Engineering to Computer Science, and excelling in both, demonstrates my ability to quickly learn and apply new skills in diverse fields.
  • Leadership: My roles in various student organizations and professional projects have equipped me with strong leadership and team collaboration skills.
  • Problem-Solving: Whether it was designing a Formula car or developing software solutions, my ability to analyze complex problems and implement effective solutions has been a cornerstone of my work.
  • Communication: My experience as an emcee and orientation leader showcases my ability to communicate effectively with diverse audiences, a skill crucial in any professional setting.
  • Community Focus: My involvement in community service through the Aditya LEO Club reflects my commitment to making a positive impact beyond my professional endeavors.
